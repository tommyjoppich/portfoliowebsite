NOAA GLANSIS Web App Redesign

Worked directly with GLANSIS to evaluate research, wireframe and prototype black and white designs, and received feedback to improve further iterations.

Summary

The goal of this project was to work directly with the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Association (NOAA) and choose a specific feature on their local GLANSIS website to redesign. While our redesigns remained in black and white, they were given feedback from other students, professors, and a representative from NOAA itself.

This was an individual class project, with checkpoints along the way for mobile designs, web app designs, and a slide deck presentation. At each checkpoint, we received feedback and evaluations which allowed for improvement and growth in the design process.

What is NOAA GLANSIS?

The GLANSIS website aims to give students, teachers, scientists, and other audiences a place to reference learn about different nonindigenous species throughout the Great Lakes region. It provides visual map aids, search features, and other resources to serve as a "one-stop shop" for any information users need.
