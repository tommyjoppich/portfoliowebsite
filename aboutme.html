About Me

As I've explored the technology industry throughout my high school and college career, I have had the opportunity to meet so many people with diverse interests, majors, and backgrounds. In doing so, I have seen projects, websites, and apps from all different angles. I fell in love wqith the creativity and expansiveness of UX Design and Front-End Coding because it allowed me to answer more open-ended questions and explore ideas from everyu view point.
When I work on design and programming projects, I really like to introduce creative solutions, even if they may not be the popular one. I have found that giving the first idea in a brainstorming session does not have to be scary, and often catalyzes the rest of the team. Throughout the past three years, I have been focusing on making these solutions accessible, usable, and innovative.
Outside of technology, I enjoy fostering many diverse hobbies. I like to play competitive Spikeball, learn not-so-competitive chess, and memorize random facts on geoguessr. I also know about one and a half card tricks, and would always like to show them!
